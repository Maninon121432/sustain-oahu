Our Mission

Preserving Paradise Through Responsible Tourism

At Sustain O'ahu, we're committed to promoting tourism practices that respect and preserve O'ahu's precious ecosystems, cultural heritage, and local communities.

We believe visitors can experience the beauty of O'ahu while contributing positively to its sustainability and helping protect this beautiful island for generations to come.

Sustainable Tourism Practices

Leave No Trace

Practice responsible tourism by taking only photographs and leaving only footprints. Pack out all trash and avoid disturbing wildlife or removing natural materials.

Carry reusable water bottles and bags
Stay on marked trails to preserve fragile ecosystems
Participate in beach or trail cleanups

Support Local Businesses

Choose locally-owned accommodations, restaurants, and tour operators. Purchase souvenirs made by local artisans and eat food grown on the islands.

Shop at farmers markets for locally grown produce
Choose restaurants that source ingredients locally
Buy authentic Hawaiian-made crafts and products
